Donor

Register: Creates a new account in the system.
Login: Accesses their individual donor account.
Book Appointment: Schedules a blood donation.
View History: Views their past donations.


Staff

Login: Accesses the system with staff privileges.
Book Appointment: Can view, manage, or update appointment statuses.
View History: Can access donation histories for donors they are processing.


Admin

Manage Inventory: Oversees blood stock levels, updates units, and tracks expiration dates.
